BVRIT HYDERABAD College of Engineering for Women

BVRIT Hyderabad College of Engineering for Women (BVRITH), Hyderabad is ranked in 201-250 band in Engineering Category by NIRF 2022 and ranked 5th at National Level Emerging Engineering Institutes in Times Engineering Ranking 2020. The institute is the youngest college under the umbrella of Sri Vishnu Educational Society. BVRITH offers degree programs in the discipline of Computer Science and Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics and Communication Engineering and many more. Admission is done on the basis of merit and cutoff criteria of the institute.
